2.1 - Page Selector

When the user successfully logged in, they would be able to select the desired page they wanted to go to.The pages that would be displayed would be based on the user type. They can either choose between Customer Management, Point of Sales, User Management (Admin Only), Inventory, Reports(Admin Only), Backup(Admin Only), Help and About.

  1. Staff side - The staff can only select from Customer Management, Point of Sales, Inventory, Help and About.
    2. The staff side screen
  2. Admin side - The admin would have additional pages besides what the staff side have and those are Report, User Management and Backup.
