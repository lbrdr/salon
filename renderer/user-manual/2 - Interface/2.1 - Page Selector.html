2.1 - Page Selector

The page selector is located at the left side of the screen. When the user successfully logged in, they would be able to select the desired page they wanted to go to within the page selector. The pages that would be displayed would be based on the user type.

They can either choose between Customer Management, Point of Sales, User Management (Admin Only), Inventory, Reports(Admin Only), Backup(Admin Only), Help and About.

    The staff side screen
  1. Staff side - The staff can only select from Customer Management, Point of Sales, Inventory, Help and About.
    2. The admin side screen
  2. Admin side - The admin would have additional pages besides what the staff side have and those are Report, User Management and Backup.
