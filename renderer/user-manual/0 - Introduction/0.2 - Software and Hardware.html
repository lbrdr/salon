0.2 - Software and Hardware Requirements

The system requires specific hardware and software to ensure optimal performance for staff and admin users.

  1. Hardware - These are the minimum hardware requirements: a processor equivalent to at least an Intel Core i3, at least 4 GB of RAM, and a minimum of 128 GB SSD storage. Additional peripherals include a monitor with a resolution of 1920x1080 or higher, a keyboard, a mouse, and backup storage like an external hard drive or network-attached storage.
