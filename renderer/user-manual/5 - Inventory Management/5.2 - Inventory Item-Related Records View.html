5.2 - Inventory Item-Related Records View

The inventory-item-related records view shows the ivnentory records that are related to a certain inventory item. Texts are provided at the left side to display details about the item such as its name, manufacturer, unit of measurement and current stock, and a table at the right side lists down the inventory records related to the viewed inventory item and their respective details.

When viewed by an admin, an edit inventory record button will be provided and a record may also be selected in the table to quickly edit an inventory record.
