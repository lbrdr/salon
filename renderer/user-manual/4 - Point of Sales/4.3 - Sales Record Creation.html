4.3 - Sales Record Creation

The sales record creation screen displays the necessary input fields needed to be filled out when making a sales record including the customer name, the preferred staff, the services availed, and the payment.

The sales record creation screen screen
  1. Customer Field - The user can input the serviced customer here. This can be left blank if the customer is not registered.
  2. Preferred Staff Field - The servicing staff for the sales record is displayed here. This selection is the current user by default and may only be changed by an admin.
  3. Transaction Date Field - The date of when the transaction happened is displayed here. This field may only be edited by an admin.
  4. Offered Services Input - The user can add, select and remove the services availed by the customer here. After selecting a service, a default price will be set that will be editable by the user.
  5. Total Price - After entering the offered services, the total price of services will be displayed here.
  6. Payment Field - The user would have to input the payment paid by the customer here.
  7. Change - After inputting the offered services and the payment, the amount of change to be received by the customer will be displayed here.
  8. Submit Button - By clicking this button, a new sales record will be created with the given information if all inputs are valid.
