4.1 - Point of Sales

The point of sales page is where the users would see sales records and its corresponding information. The point of sales page consists of the lists of sales record's information such as customer name, servicing staff responsible for doing service to the customer, and the date of when the transaction happened.

The point of sales screen
  1. Sales Record Creation - The user can click this button to proceed to create new sales record.
  2. Sales Record Details View - Upon clicking this button, the user will be able to see the services availed by the customer and when, as well as the servicing staff responsible, the total price to be paid, amount of payment paid by the customer and the change they recieve.
  3. Sales Record Edit - The user can click this button to proceed to edit sales record procedure.
  4. Services Edit - The user can click this button to proceed to the edit services procedure.
