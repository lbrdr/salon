4.1 - Point of Sales

The point of sales page is where the users would see sales records and its corresponding information. The point of sales page consists of the lists of sales record's information such as customer name, servicing staff responsible for doing service to the customer, and the date of when the transaction happened. There are also buttons that would connect you to to another page such as Edit Services, Edit Sales Record (Admin Only), Sales Record Details View and Sales Record Creation.

The point of sales screen
  1. Sales Record Creation - This button allows the user the Sales Record Creation page wherein they can create new sales record.
  2. Sales Record Details View - The user will be able to see the services availed by the customer and when, as well as the servicing staff responsible, the total price to be paid, amount of payment paid by the customer and the change they recieve.
  3. Sales Record Edit - This allows the admin to correct and edit the sales record information.
  4. Services Edit - The user can click this link to proceed to the Services Edit Page wherein the user can change information about the services.
