3.1 - Customer Management Page

The customer management page is the first screen the users would see when they have successfully logged in. The customer page consists of the lists of customers and their preffered staff along with the date of registration. There are also buttons that would connect you to to another page such as Customer Search, Edit Customer (Admin Only), Customer Related Services View and Customer Registration.

The customer management page
  1. Register Customer - This button connects the user to the Register Customer Page that allows them to register new customers.
  2. Customer Related Services View - The user will be able to see the services availed by the customer and when.
  3. Customer Search - This button connects to the Customer Search page that allows them to search customers.
  4. Edit Customer (Admin Only) - This allows the admin to correct and edit information about the registered customer.
