3.1 - Customer Management Page

The customer management page is the first screen the users would see when they have successfully logged in. This page displays lists of customers and their preffered staff along with the date of registration.

The customer management page
  1. Register Customer - The user can click this button to proceed to register new customer procedure.
  2. Customer Related Services View - Upon clicking this button, the user will be able to see the services availed by the customer and when.
  3. Customer Search - The user can click this button to proceed to search registered customers.
  4. Edit Customer - The user can click this button to proceed to edit and change customer information procedure.
