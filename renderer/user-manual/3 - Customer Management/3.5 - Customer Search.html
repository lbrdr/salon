3.5 Customer Search

This screen enables a user to search customers from the system. The user may input customer information into any of the input fields and upon clicking the search button, it would display customers that match the inputted information.

The customer search screen
  1. Customer ID Field - The user may enter the customer ID here.
  2. Full Name Field - The user may enter the customer's full name here.
  3. Contact Field - The user may enter the customer's contact information here.
  4. Preferred Staff Selection - The user may select the customer's preferred staff here. Selecting the any option would show customers regardless of whether they have a preferred staff or not. Selecting the none option would only display customers without a preferred staff.
  5. Date of Registration Fields - The user may enter the customer's date of registration here. The user will have to enter two dates where the date of registration may land in between (including the entered dates). Entering the same dates in the two fields will select customers registered within the entered date.
  6. Date of Last Service Fields - The user may enter the date of the customer's last service here. The user will have to enter two dates where the date of last service may land in between (including the entered dates). Entering the same dates in the two fields will select customers who had their last service within the entered date.
  7. Search Button - Upon clicking this button, customers that match the entered information in the input fields will be displayed in the searched customers table.
  8. Searched Customers Table - In this table, the details of the customers that pass the search are displayed. By clicking the view button in the services column, the user can view the services availed by the customer. The user may also select a customer in the table, which will be used when clicking the edit customer button.
  9. Edit Customer Button - Clicking this button allows the user to edit information about a customer. If a customer is selected in the searched customers table upon clicking this button, the information of the selected customer will be automatically entered in the customer edit fields.
